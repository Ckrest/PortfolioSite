Analytics Onboarding Sprint

A rapid prototype cycle focused on helping enterprise analysts adopt a new insights platform. We validated guidance patterns, experimented with progressive disclosure, and shipped high-fidelity flows in under two weeks.

Product Design Prototype User Research

Approach

The team mapped the first-week journey for new analysts, identifying friction around data permissions and orientation. We introduced guided tasks, inline glossary support, and a personalized summary page populated with live metrics.

By testing interactive Figma prototypes with five analysts, we confirmed that contextual prompts reduced setup time by 35% and improved confidence scores during usability sessions.

Outcome

  • Hand-off package containing screen flows, motion references, and UI kits.
  • Implementation brief for engineering including analytics event instrumentation.
  • Launch checklist covering training sessions and success metrics.
