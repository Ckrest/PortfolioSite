AI Evaluation Platform Proposal

Strategic brief delivered on September 1, 2025 that frames the vision, scope, and investment profile for our enterprise AI evaluation initiative. The document translates research findings into a roadmap that executives can act on immediately.

Strategy Research Roadmapping

Highlights

The proposal packages competitive analysis, target user journeys, and a phased rollout plan. It establishes the minimum viable evaluation toolkit, budgets for infrastructure and staffing, and a governance model for safe deployment.

Stakeholders receive a timeline with quarterly checkpoints, risk mitigation strategies, and a detailed breakdown of how measurement insights feed directly into product decisions.

Deliverables

  • 40-page executive-ready deck and narrative memo.
  • Financial model covering tooling, headcount, and operational overhead.
  • Success metrics framework aligned to compliance and customer outcomes.
