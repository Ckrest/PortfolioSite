Overview

This proposal outlines a strategic initiative for developing AI integration engineering capabilities, focusing on building modular evaluation frameworks, integration tooling, and best practices for deploying AI systems in production environments.

Key Components

  • Project milestones and timeline
  • Budget allocation and resource planning
  • Staffing requirements and team structure
  • Technical architecture and tooling recommendations
  • Risk assessment and mitigation strategies
  • Success metrics and evaluation criteria

Impact

This proposal serves as a foundational document for establishing an independent AI integration engineering practice, providing a roadmap for building scalable evaluation systems and integration frameworks that can support multiple AI initiatives.
